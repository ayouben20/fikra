Explore Fabrics

Cotton

Versatile, breathable, and easy to sew. Great for clothing, quilting, and home decor.

Linen

Strong, absorbent, and dries faster than cotton. Ideal for summer clothing and tablecloths.

Silk

Luxurious, smooth, and lustrous. Best for high-end fashion, scarves, and lingerie.

Wool

Warm, durable, and water-resistant. Used for outerwear, suits, and blankets.

Polyester

Durable, wrinkle-resistant, and quick-drying. Common in apparel, upholstery, and ropes.

Denim

Sturdy cotton twill fabric. Famous for jeans, jackets, and workwear.
